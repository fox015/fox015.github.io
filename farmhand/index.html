Farmhand: A multi-function monitor for precision agriculture

February 13, 2019

Technologies/dependencies:

Android, Java, IOIO, Gradle

Description:

FarmHand is a multifunction monitor targeted at agricultural applications. It is designed as a compliment to certain agricultural tasks including, but not limited to planting, fertiliser application, and spraying.

FarmHand is an Android application which works in tandem with IOIO hardware. Communication takes place over USB using Android OpenAccessory. While the current backend service is coupled to IOIO hardware and SDKs, the business logic is general enough that other similar hardware could be swapped out in the future.

Features:

FarmHand can be configured to track the following:

  • Time
  • Speed
  • Distance
  • Area
  • Bin/tank levels

Measurements for the above are calculated over different intervals (instantaneous, trip and job). And in units of the users choice (metric, imperial or a mix of both).
